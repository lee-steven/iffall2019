About the Song: Señorita

Señorita by Canadian singer Shawn Mendes and Cuban-American singer Camila Cabello is a song about a steamy yet ambiguous relationship (much like Shawn Mendes and Camila Cabello's past relationship). The song is Mendes' and Cabello's second collaboration song after "I Know What You Did Last Summer". Señorita was a huge success in multiple countries, hitting number one on the US Billboard Hot 100, and many others.
